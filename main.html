About Albania

Albania, located in Southeast Europe on the Balkan Peninsula, is known for its diverse culture, stunning landscapes, and historical heritage. The country offers a blend of mountains, beaches, and ancient ruins that make it a top destination for travelers.
* Capital city: Tirana
* Official language: Albanian (Shqip)
* Population:2.8 million
* Currency: Albanian lek (ALL)
* Rich history influenced by Illyrians,Ottomans
* Famous for UNESCO sites like Berat, Gjirokastër, and Butrint
* Traditional foods include byrek, tavë kosi, and fresh seafood
