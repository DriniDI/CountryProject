Welcome to Albania!

Explore the rich culture, beautiful landscapes, and vibrant history of Albania.

burek

Burek is a popular savory pastry from the Balkans made from thin, flaky phyllo dough layered with fillings like spiced ground meat, cheese, or spinach, then baked until golden and crispy, often shaped into spirals or rolls, and served with yogurt.

Tavë kosi

Tavë kosi is a traditional and national dish of Albania, a hearty casserole made of baked lamb and rice topped with a creamy, custard-like mixture of yogurt and eggs

Flia

Flia is a traditional, layered Balkan dish from Albania, resembling a savory pancake or crepe cake, made from a simple batter and cream/yogurt, slow-cooked in layers with butter,over an open fire using a special dome called saç

Bakllava

Albanian bakllava is a traditional, less sweet variation of the layered phyllo dessert, primarily made with walnuts and a simple sugar syrup. It is a holiday essential, particularly for New Year's and Eid celebrations.

Trileqe

Trileqe (also spelled Trileçe or Trilece) is a popular, light and fluffy three-milk sponge cake.

Tullumba

Tullumba its made from an unleavened choux pastry dough that is piped into short, oblong shapes (typically using a star tip), deep-fried until golden and crispy, and then immediately soaked in a cold, sweet sugar syrup.
